NEUROSYNC Static Experiment Runner

NEUROSYNC

Multimodal auditory stimulation under chromatic ambient lighting with baseline survey, acclimation, eyes-open baseline recording, verbal-semantic audio trials, visuospatial image trials, and two post-task surveys.

Flow

Baseline survey → acclimation → baseline → task 1 → survey → task 2 → survey

Audio Blocks

10 Hz during task 1, 40 Hz during task 2

Publication Note

This app is packaged as a publication-ready static experiment runner.
